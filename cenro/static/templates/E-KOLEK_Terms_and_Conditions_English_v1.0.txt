ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

1.1. "E-KOLEK" refers to the electronic waste collection platform, including its mobile application, website, and related services.

1.2. "User" refers to any individual who registers for an account on the E-KOLEK platform.

1.3. "Family Account" refers to a registered household unit consisting of a family representative and family members.

1.4. "Points" refers to the reward currency earned through waste segregation and disposal activities on the platform.

1.5. "CENRO" refers to the City Environment and Natural Resources Office of San Pedro City.

ARTICLE 2: ACCOUNT REGISTRATION

2.1. Users must provide accurate, complete, and current information during registration.

2.2. Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials.

2.3. Users must be at least 13 years old to register for an account. Users under 18 must have parental or guardian consent.

2.4. Each family may have only one family account, with one designated family representative.

2.5. Family members can join an existing family account using the family code provided by the family representative.

2.6. All registration information is subject to verification by CENRO staff.

ARTICLE 3: USER OBLIGATIONS

3.1. Users agree to:
   - Properly segregate waste according to categories defined in the platform
   - Follow the garbage collection schedule for their barangay
   - Provide accurate information about waste disposal activities
   - Maintain respectful communication with CENRO staff and other users
   - Not engage in fraudulent activities to earn points

3.2. Users must not:
   - Share account credentials with unauthorized persons
   - Create multiple accounts for the same household
   - Submit false information about waste collection or disposal
   - Attempt to manipulate the points system through fraudulent means
   - Use the platform for any illegal activities

ARTICLE 4: POINTS AND REWARDS SYSTEM

4.1. Users earn points by:
   - Proper waste segregation and disposal
   - Participating in scheduled waste collection
   - Completing educational modules about waste management
   - Participating in community cleanup activities
   - Playing educational games on the platform

4.2. Points accumulated can be redeemed for rewards as defined in the rewards catalog.

4.3. Points have no cash value and cannot be transferred between accounts.

4.4. CENRO reserves the right to adjust point values, modify the rewards catalog, and set expiration dates for points.

4.5. Points earned through fraudulent means will be forfeited, and the account may be suspended or terminated.

5.3. Personal information will be used for:
