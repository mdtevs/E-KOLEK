Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to E-KOLEK ("we," "our," or "us"). E-KOLEK is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our sustainable waste management platform and mobile application (collectively, the "Service").

By accessing or using E-KOLEK, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when registering for an account or using our Service:

  • Family Head Information: Full name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, barangay (community) location
  • Family Member Information: Full name, relationship to family head, date of birth, gender
  • Authentication Data: Username, encrypted password, security questions and answers, OTP (One-Time Password) codes
  • Account Credentials: QR codes, family codes for waste submission tracking

2.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our Service:

  • Activity Data: Waste submission records, points earned, rewards redeemed, learning module progress, game scores
  • Schedule Data: Collection schedules, pickup dates, notification preferences
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, mobile device identifiers
  • Log Data: Login attempts, access times, pages viewed, features used

2.3 Location Information

We collect your barangay (community) location to:

  • Assign appropriate waste collection schedules
  • Enable community-based leaderboards and competitions
  • Provide location-specific notifications and announcements

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

3.1 Service Delivery

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Process waste submissions and track your environmental contributions
  • Calculate and award points for sustainable waste management activities
  • Facilitate reward redemptions and manage inventory
  • Generate QR codes and family identification credentials

3.2 Communication

  • Send SMS and email notifications for collection schedules, account activities, and system updates
  • Deliver OTP codes for authentication and password resets
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send important announcements and policy changes

3.3 Platform Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve Service functionality
  • Develop new features and educational content
  • Conduct research on waste management effectiveness
  • Generate aggregated statistics and reports for environmental impact assessment

3.4 Security and Compliance

  • Monitor and prevent fraudulent activities and security breaches
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and community guidelines
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Maintain audit logs and security records

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 With Your Consent

We may share your information when you give us explicit permission, such as when participating in community challenges or public leaderboards.

4.2 Service Providers

We may share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf:

  • SMS gateway providers for OTP delivery
  • Email service providers for notifications
  • Cloud hosting and storage providers
  • Analytics and monitoring services

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for specified purposes.

4.3 Government and Regulatory Authorities

We coordinate with CENRO (City Environment and Natural Resources Office) and may share aggregated, anonymized data for:

  • Waste management policy development
  • Environmental impact assessments
  • Compliance with waste management regulations
  • Public health and safety initiatives

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental request, or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Investigate potential violations of our Terms of Service
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activities

4.5 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS/SSL in transit
  • Access Controls: Role-based access control (RBAC) limits data access to authorized personnel only
  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication (MFA) with OTP verification for account access
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (bcrypt/PBKDF2)
  • Monitoring: Continuous security monitoring and intrusion detection systems
  • Audit Logs: Comprehensive logging of all system access and data modifications
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and updates applied promptly

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but maintain industry best practices.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:

  • Active Accounts: Information is retained while your account remains active
  • Inactive Accounts: Data may be archived after 24 months of inactivity
  • Transaction Records: Waste submission and reward redemption records are retained for 5 years for audit purposes
  • Security Logs: Login attempts and security events are retained for 12 months
  • Legal Compliance: Some data may be retained longer as required by applicable laws

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us (see Section 10).

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information stored in our systems
  • Update or correct inaccurate information through your account settings
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

7.2 Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account. Please note:

  • Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Some information may be retained for legal compliance or audit purposes
  • Points and rewards will be forfeited upon account deletion

7.3 Communication Preferences

You can control notification preferences:

  • SMS notifications can be disabled through account settings
  • Email notifications can be managed via notification preferences
  • Critical security notifications cannot be disabled

7.4 Opt-Out Rights

  • Opt out of leaderboard participation (while maintaining core Service access)
  • Decline participation in surveys or research studies
  • Disable non-essential data collection features

8. Children's Privacy

E-KOLEK is designed for family use. Family members under 18 years of age may be registered by a parent or legal guardian (Family Head). We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without parental consent.

Family Heads are responsible for:

  • Providing accurate information about family members
  • Supervising minors' use of the Service
  • Managing family account security and privacy settings

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13 without proper consent, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated Privacy Policy with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Sending email notifications to registered users
  • Displaying prominent in-app notifications
  • Requiring acknowledgment of changes upon next login (for material changes)

Your continued use of the Service after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

11. Philippine Data Privacy Act Compliance

E-KOLEK complies with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) and its implementing rules and regulations. As a data subject, you have the following rights under Philippine law:

  • Right to be informed about data collection and processing
  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to object to processing of personal data
  • Right to erasure or blocking of data
  • Right to rectify inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission

For concerns related to your data privacy rights under Philippine law, you may contact the National Privacy Commission at:

National Privacy Commission

Email: info@privacy.gov.ph

Website: www.privacy.gov.ph
